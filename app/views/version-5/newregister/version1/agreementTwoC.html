Agreement between you and the ESFA

This legal agreement sets out how apprenticeship funding will work between you, the ESFA and your training provider.

What is in the agreement

You must read and understand the agreement and be aware of the following:

  • you agree to pay co-investment costs for your apprenticeship training
  • you must have a contract with your training provider
  • if you don’t follow the rules of this agreement, you might be asked to pay the funding back and to pay for the rest of the training
  • if the ESFA suspects that the agreement or funding rules are not being followed, you must help with the investigation

    • If you pay the apprenticeship levy:

  • you can only use your levy funds to pay for apprenticeship training and assessment
